Scale Simulator


The scale simulator factbox provides a simple emulation tool that can be used to return (via a RestAPI) a weight to Business Central. The Max Wieght can be used to adjust the slider controsl upper value, and the slider can be used to adjust the weight returned. When the Max Weight is 100 or less the slider works in increments of 1, if the max weight is less than 1000 the slider increments in 10, a max weight of more that 1000 will increment in intervals of 100
